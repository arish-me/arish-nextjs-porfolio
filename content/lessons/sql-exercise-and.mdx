## Exercise: Multiple Conditions

Find students in grade A AND aged 14.

## Solution

```sql
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE grade = 'A'
AND age = 14;
```

## Practice More

```sql
-- JavaScript students with marks above 75
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE course = 'JavaScript'
AND marks > 75;

-- Grade B students older than 12
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE grade = 'B'
AND age > 12;
```
