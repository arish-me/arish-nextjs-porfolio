## NOT BETWEEN

Excludes values within a range.

## Syntax

```sql
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE age NOT BETWEEN 12 AND 14;
```

## Example

Students NOT aged 12-14:

```sql
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE age NOT BETWEEN 12 AND 14;
```

Returns students aged 11 or below, and 15 or above.

## With Marks

```sql
-- Marks outside 70-80 range
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE marks NOT BETWEEN 70 AND 80;
```
