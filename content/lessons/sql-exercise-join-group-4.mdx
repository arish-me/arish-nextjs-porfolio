## Exercise: Count with HAVING

Courses with more than 5 students.

## Solution

```sql
SELECT c.course_name, COUNT(*) AS count
FROM students s
JOIN courses c ON s.course_id = c.id
GROUP BY c.course_name
HAVING COUNT(*) > 5;
```
