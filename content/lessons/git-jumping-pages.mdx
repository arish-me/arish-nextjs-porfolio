## Jumping Between Pages

Sometimes you need to jump directly to a specific lesson or chapter. Here's how to do it efficiently.

## Using the Course Menu

Click on the course title in the navigation to see all chapters and lessons. From there, you can:

- Click any chapter to expand it
- Click any lesson to jump directly to it
- See your progress for each section

## Using the Progress Tracker

The sidebar shows your progress:

- ✅ **Completed lessons** - Green checkmark
- ⭕ **Current lesson** - Highlighted
- ○ **Upcoming lessons** - Empty circle

## Quick Jump Tips

### Jump to Specific Content

1. Use the chapter dropdown to find topics
2. Click lesson titles to navigate directly
3. Use browser back/forward buttons

### Bookmarking

You can bookmark any lesson URL to return to it later:

```
https://yoursite.com/courses/git-github-essentials/chapter-slug/lesson-slug
```

## Finding What You Need

If you're looking for something specific:

1. **Sequential Learning** - Go through lessons in order
2. **Reference** - Jump to specific topics as needed
3. **Review** - Revisit completed lessons anytime

## Your Progress is Saved

Don't worry about losing your place! Your progress is automatically saved, so you can:

- Close the browser and return later
- Pick up exactly where you left off
- Review any lesson at any time

Now that you know how to navigate, let's start learning Git!
