## Exercise: Sum by Instructor

Total marks taught by each instructor.

## Solution

```sql
SELECT i.name, SUM(s.marks) AS total
FROM instructors i
JOIN courses c ON i.id = c.instructor_id
JOIN students s ON s.course_id = c.id
GROUP BY i.name;
```
