## Exercise: Students in Popular Courses

Find students in courses with > 5 enrollments.

## Solution

```sql
SELECT * FROM students
WHERE course_id IN (
  SELECT course_id FROM students
  GROUP BY course_id HAVING COUNT(*) > 5
);
```
