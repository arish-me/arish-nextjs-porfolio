## Exercise: Three Tables

Join students, courses, and departments.

## Solution

```sql
SELECT s.name, c.course_name, d.dept_name
FROM students s
JOIN courses c ON s.course_id = c.id
JOIN departments d ON c.dept_id = d.id;
```
